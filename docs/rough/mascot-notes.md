# Bills App Mascot Notes

## Primary direction

- Goth girl mascot in an Arcane-inspired painterly 3D/2D hybrid style.
- Mood: confident, slightly mysterious, calm rather than aggressive.
- Visual motifs: ledger pages, ink, receipts, coins, or a subtle bill/statement motif.

## Other possible avatar directions

- Piggy bank with cracked ceramic and gold kintsugi lines (savings + repair).
- Origami crow made from a utility bill (sharp, smart, minimalist).
- Small golem made of stacked receipts (structured, reliable, friendly).

## Style considerations

- Keep the silhouette readable at small sizes (app icon / avatar).
- Avoid heavy detail in the face; favor bold shapes and strong lighting.
- Maintain a consistent 2-3 color accent palette for brand cohesion.

## Additional character brainstorm (same style)

- Lanky ink-cat familiar carrying a tiny envelope satchel.
- Stoic raven courier with a wax-seal pouch and a receipt-scroll tail.
- Gentle “ledger spirit” made of floating paper fragments and subtle glow eyes.
- Fox accountant with a long coat and an abacus charm (smart, nimble).
- Streetwise moth girl with receipt-patterned wings and a small coin purse.
